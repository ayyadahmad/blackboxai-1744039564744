Frequently Asked Questions

Turnaround time varies depending on the complexity of the project. Simple emotes typically take 3-5 days, while detailed illustrations can take 1-2 weeks.

I provide artwork in PNG, JPEG, and PSD formats. For emotes, I also provide transparent PNG files suitable for Twitch and Discord.

Each package includes a specific number of revision rounds. During each round, you can request changes to ensure the artwork meets your vision.

I accept PayPal, credit cards, and bank transfers. A 50% deposit is required to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon completion.
